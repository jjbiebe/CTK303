In Da Club by 50 Cent

About the Song

In Da Club is a lead single from 50 Cent's debut studio album "Get Rich or Die Tryin'". It is kinda humorous considering the opening line is "Go, shawty, it's your birthday" and this being the birthday song for this assignment. Either way, the track lasted 9 weeks in the number one spot on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and is highly acclaimed.

Released: January 7, 2003
